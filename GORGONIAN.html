🪸 Gorgonian Sea Fan Coral

📌 Overview

Gorgonian sea fan (Gorgonia ventalina) is a soft coral with a flat, fan-like structure.

  • Belongs to octocorals
  • Flexible branching structure
  • Common in Caribbean reefs
  • Supports marine habitats

👉 Designed to capture food from water currents.

🌍 Habitat

📍 Location:

  • Caribbean Sea
  • Western Atlantic Ocean

🌊 Conditions:

  • Depth: 5–30 m
  • Temperature: 24–28°C
  • Moderate to strong currents

🪸 Type:

  • Reef slopes
  • Shallow reefs
  • Hard substrates

👉 Positioned to maximize feeding efficiency.

🎯 Fun Facts

  • 🪭 Fan-like appearance
  • 🌊 Faces currents for feeding
  • 🌿 Flexible and bendable
  • 🌞 Uses algae + active feeding
  • 🎨 Bright purple, red, yellow
  • 🐠 Shelter for marine life

⚠️ Threats

  • 🌡️ Climate change
  • 🦠 Sea fan disease
  • 🌊 Ocean acidification
  • 🏭 Pollution
  • 🚤 Human damage

🌱 Conservation

  • 🌊 Marine protected areas
  • 🔬 Research
  • 🌍 Climate action
  • 🧍 Awareness programs
